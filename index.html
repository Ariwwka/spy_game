How to play

  1. Pick the number of players and tap Start Game.
  2. The game secretly picks one famous character. Most players get that character — some randomly become a SPY.
  3. Pass the phone around. Each player privately reveals their card, then hides it.
  4. Spies only see the word SPY — they don't know the character!
  5. Discuss and ask questions to find the spies. Spies try to blend in.

There's no fixed number of spies — a round might have none, one, or many. Every player has a 30% chance of being a spy.
